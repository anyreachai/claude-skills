# Narrative Deck Playbook

Build compelling narrative decks using the "Save Your Deck" framework — reverse-engineered from Emergence Water, Elektra Health, and Fronteo decks.

## Three Modes

Select the mode based on your audience:

| Mode | Audience | Emotional Lever | Reference |
|------|----------|----------------|-----------|
| **Mode A: Outrage** | BPO partners, ops/finance buyers | Shock at industry dysfunction | `references/mode-a-outrage.md` |
| **Mode B: Empathy** | Enterprise prospects (NWFCU, Centene, Fabletics) | "We understand your pain" | `references/mode-b-empathy.md` |
| **Mode C: Paradigm** | Series A investors, analysts | "The world is shifting" | `references/mode-c-paradigm.md` |

## Workflow

1. **Ask the user**: Who is the audience? This determines the mode.
2. **Read the appropriate mode reference file** for the full framework.
3. **Storyboard first**: Lock the narrative in plain text before any design.
4. **Apply universal principles**: One-idea-per-slide, bold-text reading path, progressive commitment, 4-act emotional arc.
5. **Hand off to shadcn-deck** for visual execution if needed.

## Universal Principles (Apply to ALL Modes)

- **One-Idea-Per-Slide**: If you can't summarize in one sentence, split it.
- **Bold-Text Reading Path**: Bold words form a readable sentence for C-suite skimmers.
- **Progressive Commitment**: Small truths early → harder to reject bigger claims later.
- **Storyboard-First**: Story must work in plain text before touching design.
- **4-Act Emotional Arc**: Broken World → Nothing Works → We Figured It Out → Join Us.
